## Feature Prioritization Framework

### MoSCoW for Each Quarter

**Must Have**: Launch blockers, core value prop
**Should Have**: Significant value add, low effort
**Could Have**: Nice to have, medium effort
**Won't Have**: Deferred to future

### Prioritization Criteria

| Criterion | Weight |
|-----------|--------|
| User value | 30% |
| Business value | 25% |
| Effort (inverse) | 20% |
| Strategic alignment | 15% |
| Risk reduction | 10% |

## Dependencies & Risks

### External Dependencies
| Dependency | Risk | Mitigation |
|------------|------|------------|
| Supabase reliability | Medium | Monitoring, backup plan |
| Payment processor approval | Medium | Apply early, backup options |
| School budget cycles | High | Align with academic year |
| Content translation | Medium | Build translator network |

### Key Risks
| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Low user adoption | Medium | High | Strong content marketing, SEO |
| Schools won't pay | Medium | High | Prove B2C value first |
| Competitor enters | Low | Medium | Move fast, build moat |
| Content quality issues | Medium | Medium | Review process, feedback loop |
